**Every τ fails G4 (substituted-primary OOF > current).** All gates G1, G2, G3 pass for τ=0.97/0.99 (and G2 fails for τ=0.95 only on standalone errs). G4 universally fails.

## Diagnosis: Pareto-frontier

The standalone +0.00035 OOF lift from τ=0.95 evaporates when blended into the primary at 0.35 weight in the 3-way (then further diluted at 0.7 of stack-2 + 0.7 of final blend → effective ~17% of primary's log-prob mass).

Per-class deltas reveal the trade: substituting any τ produces FEWER total errors on Low/Medium (−2 to −67 errors) at the cost of LOWER High recall (−0.0003 to −0.0007). Under macro-recall, High has 3× leverage (1/n_H vs 1/n_L,M proportionally), so total-error reduction in the Low/Medium space doesn't compensate for High recall loss.

This is the same Pareto-frontier closure pattern documented in earlier audits (disagree-meta-stack 2026-04-24, selective-router 2026-04-24, missed-High detector 2026-04-24, per-bin blend 2026-04-25). The primary's per-class recall trade [0.9955, 0.9695, 0.9775] is on the macro-recall-optimal Pareto frontier given the existing OOF bank — moving error mass from High → Low/Medium hurts even when it reduces total error count.

## Implication

**Lever closed**. τ-sweep on stage-1 pseudo is null because:
1. The standalone lift is real but doesn't survive blending dilution (~17% effective weight in primary).
2. The error-redistribution favors Low/Medium at expense of High — the wrong direction for macro-recall.

**No LB probe.** Primary `submission_tier1b_greedy_meta.csv` unchanged at LB 0.98094.

## Portable rule

(Logged for LEARNINGS.md future-comp playbook): **"A standalone OOF lift from a single component does not reliably translate into a blended-pipeline lift, even at substitution-not-addition. When the pipeline is a deep stack (4+ blend layers), the effective weight of any single component is tiny; the standalone lift dilutes by the stack depth × downstream weights."** Rule of thumb: if substitution doesn't lift the FINAL stack OOF by at least 30% of the standalone Δ, the lever is null.
