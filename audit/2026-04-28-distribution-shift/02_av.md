# 02 — Adversarial validation: orig (0) vs synth-train (1)

5-fold XGB on a balanced concat of orig (10k) + synth-train-subsample
(10k) using only target-FREE features (8 cats factorized + 11 raw
numerics + 4 rule indicators + 5 decimal-fraction features = 28
columns). Heavy reg, depth=4, ES=100, 2000-round cap.

## Result

| diagnostic                  | value     |
|-----------------------------|-----------|
| Overall OOF AUC (5-fold)    | **0.69690** |
| Mean fold AUC               | 0.69722   |
| Per-fold AUCs               | 0.6953 / 0.6909 / 0.6920 / 0.7059 / 0.7020 |
| Per-fold best_iter          | 304 / 414 / 678 / 756 / 776 |

AUC 0.697 means a target-free classifier can distinguish orig from
synth-train with ~70% accuracy. **This is a strong joint shift.**
For comparison:

| AV pair                          | AUC      | shift       |
|----------------------------------|----------|-------------|
| train ↔ test (J3, 2026-04-25)    | 0.50247  | none        |
| **orig ↔ synth-train**           | **0.69690** | substantial |

So the synth's relationship to its OWN test split is statistically
indistinguishable, but its relationship to the ORIGINAL anchor is
strongly distinguishable. This is the joint-level confirmation of the
marginal Rainfall_mm finding.

## What this means

Three observations land:

1. **`norain` carries more gain than `Rainfall_mm` itself.** Trees can
   distinguish the two sources better with the binary `Rainfall_mm <
   300` indicator than with the raw value. The synth's Rainfall shift
   is concentrated at the threshold — it **moved mass across the
   `<300` boundary**, not just rescaled the whole distribution.

2. **`dry` and `windy` rule indicators rank above their raw numerics
   (Soil_Moisture, Wind_Speed_kmh).** Same threshold-crossing pattern
   on the dry / windy axes, just smaller magnitude than Rainfall.

3. **Decimal-fraction features (e.g., Sunlight_Hours_dec, Soil_pH_dec)
   appear in top-15.** This means the synth's NN preserves coarse
   distributions but introduces **digit-level fingerprints** that the
   classifier picks up. Consistent with the 2026-04-22 digit-extraction
   FE breakthrough (LB +0.00116 at the time): synthetic float values
   carry quantization signatures. Here we see those signatures are also
   different from the original's digit distributions.
