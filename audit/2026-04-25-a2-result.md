# A2 result: Greedy with `b2_groupkfold_crop` in pool — NULL on intent, OOF-inflation via pathological components

**Goal**: test whether the GroupKFold-by-Crop OOF carries orthogonal signal worth blending into the meta-stacker pipeline.

**Setup**: re-ran `tier1b_greedy_with_meta.py` (which auto-loads any `oof_*.npy` not in the EXCLUDE list). The pool grew from the original 30+ components to 78 base components (incl. `b2_groupkfold_crop` and the parallel session's cross-pollinate metas `xgb_metastack_v3 / varB / varC`).

**`b2_groupkfold_crop` was NEVER selected at any step.** The original A2 hypothesis (Crop-grouped OOF as orthogonal signal) is falsified by greedy. Crop's errors are dominated by the existing pool's coverage — no new lift to extract.

## Why the +0.00050 OOF lift is OOF-inflation, not LB signal

Both selected components are documented sources of OOF-inflation that doesn't transfer:

1. **`xgb_metastack_v3__iso`** is the parallel session's cross-pollinate meta-stacker — itself trained on a bank that **includes the original `xgb_metastack`**. Recursive meta-on-meta. CLAUDE.md (Tier 1b cross-pollinate session, 2026-04-25) shows v3's standalone iso = 0.98121 but the rule reconfirmed there is *"meta-stacker ensembling at fixed pool size is bounded by what the v1 meta already extracts"*. Adding it at α=0.500 amplifies recursive stacking artifact.

2. **`recipe_focal_g2_aH1`** is the focal-loss XGB explicitly closed NULL in CLAUDE.md (focal-loss session, 2026-04-22). Magnitude-trap: 36,942 errors at recipe bias vs anchor's 9,873 (3.7× more). At α=0.150 it pulls the blend toward focal's High-class-shifted decision surface, producing 71 more High predictions on test (10184 vs 10113 in the LB-verified primary). Each false-flip-to-High costs 1/239k Medium recall while each true-flip-to-High wins 1/21k High recall — under macro-recall the trade has a sharp break-even (8.8% precision); focal's component-level precision on these flips isn't established.

3. The blend OOF→LB calibration ladder shows OOF lifts above the +0.00020 transfer threshold transfer at ratios 0.4-1.0 (see CLAUDE.md ladder). But every prior submission with **recursive meta** or **magnitude-trap component** has either LB-regressed (binhigh +0.00086 OOF → −0.00148 LB) or sat at the structural ceiling (4-way 0.98050 OOF → ~0.97995 LB).

## Implication for next-steps

The greedy script's behavior under an expanded pool reveals a real concern: **with recursive metas and known-pathological components on disk, greedy will find OOF-inflation paths that pass the +2e-4 gate**. The gate alone is insufficient when the pool includes derived components.

**Action item for future greedy runs**: extend `EXCLUDE_FROM_POOL` to include all meta variants (`xgb_metastack_v3`, `varB`, `varC`, `bag3`, `v2`) AND the magnitude-trap focal/distill components (`recipe_focal_g2_aH1`, `recipe_focal_g2h3`, `soft_distill_small`, `soft_distill_tiny`). Only original base learners + iso copies should be in the pool.

A2 net: **null on intent; +1 portable rule on greedy pool hygiene.**
