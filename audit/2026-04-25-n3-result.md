## Diagnosis

same mechanism (audit follow-up, A1 τ-sweep, A2 GroupKFold-Crop, N3
5-shuffle):

> Standalone OOF lift exists, Jaccard < 0.85 looks promising, errs ≤
> anchor passes magnitude gate, but the substitution into the primary
> redistributes error mass *off* High recall — wrong direction under
> macro-recall (High has 3× leverage as the 3.3% rare class).

per-class trade `[0.9955, 0.9695, 0.9775]` is on the macro-recall-
optimal frontier given the existing OOF bank. Any lever that
redistributes error mass from High to Low/Medium hurts macro-recall
even when it reduces total error count.
